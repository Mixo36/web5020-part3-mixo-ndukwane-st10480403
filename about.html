About Morning Cloud Cafe:

Founding:

Morning Cloud Cafe was found by entrepreneur Mixo Ndukwane in the early 2023, Mixo was financially funded by his parents and his savings to start the cafe. Morning Cloud Cafe started as a dream to uplift the coffee experience in the community, he wanted to build a place where people feel comfortable, accepted, and at ease, while the name “Morning Cloud Cafe” was motivated by the Calm, soft, peaceful skies you often see in the early morning.

The Beginning:

Morning Cloud Cafe is located in [ Gqeberha, Humewood,17 strand avenue], the cafe started opening its doors in June 2023 whereby they were giving free cups of coffee, quickly becoming a neighborhood's favorite coffee shop. From the start Mixo worked hard to create and maintain the coffee shop creating a peaceful place where people feel welcome and comfortable by having Soft, comfortable chairs and couches, gentle lighting, having calm and soft colors like light blue and white used to decorate the cafe, the colors and look reminded people of the gentle sky early in the morning.

Vision:

Through Morning Cloud Cafe, Mixo aimed to offer more than just a cup of coffee. The cafe became a place for students, professionals, and creatives anyone seeking a peaceful place to start their day.

Community Connection:

Beyond its calm and gentle setting, Morning Cloud Cafe quickly became a very important part of the community life. Regular patrons were greeted by name, local artists displayed their work on the walls, and the cafe hosted monthly morning poetry readings and casual “cloud-watch” gatherings. These activities made the cafe a gentle place for connection and reflection for the community.

Mission

Our mission at Morning Cloud Cafe:

is to create a warm and welcoming space where every cup of coffee brings comfort, connection, and joy, we are willing to serve high-quality brews and fresh pastries, which is motivated by the calm of early morning skies, while encouraging a sense of togetherness amongst locals,students, and visitors
